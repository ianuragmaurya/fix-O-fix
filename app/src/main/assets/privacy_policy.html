Privacy Policy

Welcome to FixoFix. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle user information in our application.

Information We Collect

FixoFix is a login and registration based application. We may collect the following information:

  • Name, email, and phone number during registration
  • Login credentials
  • Address details for service booking
  • Basic device and usage information

How We Use Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide home services like furniture repair, AC repair, home cleaning, and other appliance services
  • Process bookings and orders (add to cart / buy services)
  • Improve app performance and user experience

Internet Permission

FixoFix requires internet access to connect users with service providers and process requests.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Information may be shared only with service professionals required to complete your booking.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user data, but no system is 100% secure.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.
